What It Does

Four core capabilities to evaluate public goods projects at scale.

Trust Graph Analysis

Map donor-project relationships with Jaccard similarity, Shannon entropy, sybil detection, and whale dependency scoring. Identify coordinated donation patterns and flag suspicious behavior across the entire funding network.

Deep AI Evaluation

Claude Opus 4.6 scores projects across 8 dimensions: Impact, Team, Innovation, Sustainability, Ecosystem Fit, Transparency, Community Engagement, and Risk Assessment. Each evaluation produces actionable recommendations.

PDF Intelligence Reports

Generate branded, watermarked PDF reports with executive summaries, quantitative analysis, trust graph visualizations, mechanism comparisons, and prioritized recommendations for each project.

Mechanism Simulation

Compare four QF variants side-by-side: Standard, Capped, Equal-Weight, and Trust-Weighted. Analyze Gini coefficients, top-share concentration, and fairness metrics to design better funding mechanisms.
